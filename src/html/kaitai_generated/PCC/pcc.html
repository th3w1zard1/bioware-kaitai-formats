Pcc format specification

Type: Pcc

**PCC** (Mass Effect–era Unreal package): BioWare variant of UE packages — `file_header`, name/import/export tables, then export blobs. May be zlib/LZO chunked (`bioware_pcc_compression_codec` in `bioware_common`). **Not KotOR:** no `k1_win_gog_swkotor.exe` grounding — follow LegendaryExplorer wiki + `meta.xref`.
